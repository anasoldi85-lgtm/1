About ZDesk PRO

Founded in Brighton & Hove, United Kingdom, ZDesk PRO is a leading specialized financial information consulting firm focusing on global financial analysis, asset planning and market research services. With over a decade of industry experience, we have established a reputation for excellence in delivering precise and actionable financial insights.

Who We Are

Established with headquarters at Brighton & Hove, England, ZDesk PRO consists of senior financial analysts, market researchers and investment consultants with decades of cross-border financial industry experience. Our team combines academic expertise with practical market knowledge to deliver solutions that drive tangible results.

We focus on providing accurate financial market data interpretation, investment strategy planning, risk assessment and customized financial information consulting services to global clients from all industry sectors, including fintech, real estate, manufacturing, healthcare, and more.
